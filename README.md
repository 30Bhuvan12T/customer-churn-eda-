# 📊 Customer Churn Analysis – Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

## 📌 Project Overview

Customer retention is one of the most important factors influencing business growth and profitability. This project performs an in-depth Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on a customer subscription dataset to identify the key factors contributing to customer churn.

The analysis investigates customer demographics, subscription plans, complaints, customer satisfaction (CSAT), contract types, monthly charges, Customer Lifetime Value (CLTV), and cancellation behavior. The insights generated are translated into actionable business recommendations that can help improve customer retention and long-term revenue.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Clean and prepare the dataset for analysis.
* Explore customer behavior using statistical summaries and visualizations.
* Identify the major factors influencing customer churn.
* Analyze customer satisfaction, complaints, subscription plans, and revenue.
* Generate business insights and actionable recommendations to improve customer retention.

## 🔍 Key Business Insights

* Customers with complaint histories exhibit significantly higher churn rates than customers without complaints.
* Lower Customer Satisfaction (CSAT) scores are strongly associated with increased cancellation rates.
* Short-term contract customers are more likely to churn than customers with long-term contracts.
* Customer Lifetime Value (CLTV) differs substantially between retained and cancelled customers.
* Monthly charges influence customer retention, with higher-priced subscriptions showing different churn patterns.
* Revenue contribution varies across subscription plans, highlighting opportunities for pricing and product optimization.
* Complaint and escalation trends provide valuable indicators for identifying customers at risk of churn.

---

## 💼 Business Recommendations

* Improve complaint resolution time through proactive customer support.
* Prioritize customers with low CSAT scores using personalized retention campaigns.
* Encourage customers to adopt long-term subscription plans through loyalty incentives.
* Regularly monitor high-risk customers using churn indicators and behavioral metrics.
* Focus marketing efforts on customer segments that combine high Customer Lifetime Value (CLTV) with strong retention.
* Continuously monitor customer feedback and service quality to reduce preventable churn.
